CenTrak Certified — The Partner Standard
It is not just training — it is a credential earned only when both knowledge and competence are proven. The CenTrak Certified badge signals your team has been validated, not just instructed, and can be relied on to deliver safely, effectively, and consistently.
🗻 5-Stage Certification Framework
1Ignite
🔥 Foundation
2Immerse
🎓 ILT Training
3Validate
✅ Assessment
4Certify
🏅 Credential
5Evolve
🔄 Annual Recert
Ignite: Self-paced eLearning builds foundational knowledge before ILT
Immerse: Virtual or live ILT with guided labs and structured exercises
Validate: Formal certification assessment — exams, practical exercises, or scenario-based evaluations. Conducted within the certification program, not tied to a live customer project.
Certify: Earn the official CenTrak Certified credential after completing all required validations and assessments — confirming you are prepared to deliver consistent, high-quality deployments.
Evolve: Annual recertification — mandatory to maintain certified status

🏢 Employee Learning Pathways

🆕 All Employee Learning Pathways are FREE — access at training.centrak.com using your CenTrak employee login.
What It Is
A structured, role-based training approach that gives each employee exactly the knowledge they need — no more, no less — based on their responsibilities. Training is organized by solution and depth.
Why It Matters
Ensures every employee understands CenTrak solutions at the right level for their role, enabling better collaboration across teams and stronger customer interactions — without requiring full field certification.
⚡ How It Works — Three Stages
1
Foundation
Open to all CenTrak employees
Provides baseline awareness of CenTrak solutions, their purpose, and their impact in healthcare. Sets a common vocabulary and understanding across all teams.
2
Product Knowledge
Support • Project Management • Managed Services
System-level knowledge of features, workflows, and deployment context — assigned based on role-specific needs to teams who need deeper understanding to support customers effectively.
3
Advanced
Support • Technical PMs • Deeper Expertise Roles
Advanced product knowledge and troubleshooting skills for teams directly assisting deployments and customers — Support, Technical Project Managers, and roles requiring higher technical proficiency.
